About us  /  01

Established in 1987, our practice has a long-standing presence in Surrey and Greater London, with our offices being based in Esher, Surrey. We specialise in crafting bespoke residential designs tailored for private clients. Our approach centres around creating liveable, functional spaces that our clients can call a home. By deeply understanding the lifestyle and aspirations of our clients, we strive to design homes that not only reflect architectural value but also enrich daily living. While we are respectful of the history and style of existing buildings, we are equally passionate about exploring innovative and exciting ways to design for modern life.

About Porous Delab

Porous Delab is a global architecture studio working across architecture, interiors, product design, and visualization to create immersive environments where built form connects deeply with emotion and experience. We approach every project with creativity and technical precision, ensuring designs that are both timeless and contemporary.

Our Philosophy

Creativity, Construction, Collaboration

Committed to both traditional architecture and modern innovation, our expertise in classical and traditional architecture informs our contemporary work. This allows us to create spaces that honour the past while pushing the boundaries of form and material. We are dedicated to balancing aesthetic appeal with functional needs, ensuring every design is beautiful, purposeful, and timeless. We remain closely involved throughout the project, from initial concept to completion, ensuring every detail is crafted to bring our clients’ vision to life.

Creativity

Blending classical principles with contemporary innovation, we design buildings and create spaces that are both inspiring and functional, tailored to reflect your vision. Our approach integrates timeless aesthetics with modern solutions, creating environments that are both elegant and practical.

Construction

Detailing is at the heart of our practice. Through comprehensive construction information, we uphold high standards of precision and accuracy. Our dedication to thorough planning and execution achieves both beauty and lasting quality in every project.

Collaboration

We work closely with clients, contractors, and consultants, leveraging our experience and expertise for a smooth and successful project. Our strong network and hands-on approach enhance efficiency, ensuring every aspect of the project meets the highest standards.
