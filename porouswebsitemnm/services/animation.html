Our Work Process

[sctn-03]
[stg-01]

Client Meeting

We start with a detailed discussion of your project—its goals, format, and key aspects to be highlighted in the 3D architectural animation. We determine the target audience, intended use (presentation, advertisement, social media), and the visual style that best conveys the concept.
[stg-02]

Gathering and Structuring the Technical Brief

Based on our discussion, we create a comprehensive technical brief containing all necessary details: concept, script ideas, stylistic direction, key visual and textual elements. The client’s input is crucial in defining the core message of the video, its target audience, and any specific phrases, slogans, or highlights that should align with the visuals.
[stg-03]

Script Development

We design the storyline, sequence of scenes, and the overall rhythm of the 3D animation rendering. Every frame should not only be visually striking but also contribute to a cohesive narrative.
[stg-04]

Storyboarding

Before animating, we create a storyboard in the form of static images. This provides a clear visual preview of the 3D-rendering video, showcasing camera angles, compositions, and visual effects. The storyboard and script are then presented to the client for approval, allowing for revisions at this stage.
[stg-05]

Test Video & Revisions

Before finalizing, we produce a test segment of the 3D animation video. This gives the client an opportunity to request modifications, which we meticulously refine to achieve a flawless result.

What types of 3D animation do you offer?

More

We create a wide range of 3D animation videos designed for architects, developers, designers, and brands.

Architectural Animation

Used to visualize interiors and exteriors, helping to present architectural projects, residential and commercial buildings, and convey the atmosphere of a space before its realization. Ideal for presentations, marketing materials, and real estate sales.

Product Animation

Designed for advertising products, such as furniture brands and interior items. It allows for showcasing products in a perfect setting, highlighting their functionality and design.

We also differentiate 3D animation rendering based on the technology used: Full CG (Full Computer-Generated) – The entire video is fully created in 3D software, providing complete freedom in camera angles, lighting, and animation, ensuring cinematic quality.

Depending on your goals, we will select the optimal type of architectural animation services and the best technologies for your visual content.

[qstn-02]

What is the turnaround time for a 3D animation project?

More

Timelines depend on the scale and complexity of the 3D animation video:

  • Product animation (e.g., a sofa animation) – 1 to 2 weeks
  • Architectural animation (e.g., a residential complex over 1 minute long) – 4 weeks or more, depending on detail level, number of scenes, and complex animation elements
  • Smaller animation projects – around 2 weeks

Each project is discussed individually, and we always agree on realistic deadlines in advance to ensure the highest quality final product.

[qstn-03]

What software and technologies do you use for 3D animation?

More

For 3D animation rendering, we use professional software and cutting-edge technologies:

  • 3ds Max, Blender – for 3D modeling and creating detailed objects
  • Unreal Engine – for scene assembly, materials, lighting, and real-time effects
  • After Effects, DaVinci Resolve – for post-production, compositing, color grading, and final video processing

These tools enable us to produce realistic 3D videos with high detail and cinematic quality.

[qstn-04]

How do you calculate the cost of a 3D animation project?

More

The cost depends on the scale and complexity of the project and is always calculated individually after reviewing the technical requirements. To get a quote for our architectural animation services, simply contact us in a way that’s most convenient for you.
