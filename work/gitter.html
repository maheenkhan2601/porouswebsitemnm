Multifunctional skyscraper
[inf-03]

Location

Italy, Roma

The urban landscape unfolds in precise geometric lines of contemporary architecture. At the center of the composition, a glass skyscraper rises—a concise yet expressive landmark that sets the rhythm for the surrounding space. Its facade, with a carefully designed modular structure, reflects the light and dynamic flow of the metropolis, creating an effect of transparency and lightness while maintaining a sense of monumentality.

The architectural environment is shaped by a cohesive ensemble of buildings, all adhering to a unified aesthetic. The predominant materials—glass, metal, and concrete—emphasize the technological sophistication and functionality of the structures. Horizontal and vertical elements create a balance between scale and detail, while facade solutions range from fully transparent surfaces to combined structures featuring metal and concrete inserts.
