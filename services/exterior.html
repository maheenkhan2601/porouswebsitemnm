Our Work Process

[sctn-03]
[stg-01]

Client Meeting

We begin by discussing project details, scale, and key aspects with the client. For example, in a 3D architectural visualization project, we clarify the number of views, level of detail, and specific requests.
[stg-02]

Technical Analysis

We review all provided materials, identifying what is available and what additional resources are needed. This ensures a clear understanding of the project requirements from the start, leading to high-quality architectural renders that meet all client expectations.
[stg-03]

Concept Development

We provide initial black-and-white renders to define the optimal composition and perspective before finalizing materials and lighting.
[stg-04]

Color Rendering & Refinement

Once the views are approved, we add materials, textures, lighting, and environmental elements, creating a realistic and visually appealing architectural render.
[stg-05]

Final Deliverables

After approval, we produce the final high-resolution 3D renders, ready for presentation. Whether it's a residential complex, office building, or commercial space, our visualizations capture all design nuances and advantages.

FAQ

Your questions, answered
[qstn-01]

How much does 3D architectural rendering cost?

More

The cost depends on project complexity, the number of required views, and deadlines. Contact us for a precise quote.

[qstn-02]

What are the four types of architectural rendering?

More
  1. Aerial Visualization – Shows the structure from above, including the surrounding environment.
  2. Exterior Rendering – Focuses on the facade, materials, and surroundings.
  3. Interior Visualization – Showcases interior design, lighting, and textures.
  4. 360° Panoramic Rendering – Allows interactive exploration from different angles.
[qstn-03]

What makes a great architectural render?

More

A top-quality render should be photorealistic, highly detailed, and expertly composed with proper lighting, materials, and textures. Perspective, composition, and atmosphere are key to conveying the project's essence and making it visually appealing to clients and investors.

[qstn-04]

How long does it take to create an architectural visualization?

More

Timelines depend on project complexity, the number of views, and required detailing. Simple renders can be completed in a few days, while complex projects take longer. Contact us for an exact timeframe.

[qstn-05]

What materials do you need to start an architectural visualization project?

More

To begin, we require the following:

  • Concept References – Interior/exterior references, material samples, texture images, and environmental photos.
  • 3D Models – Architectural 3D models in formats (.fbx, .3ds, .obj, .max, .skp, .rvt).
  • Blueprints – Architectural drawings in PDF or CAD format.
[qstn-06]

What is the approval process for architectural visualization?

More

The approval process includes 2-3 rounds of revisions. We start with black-and-white drafts to finalize composition, then move to colored renders with textures and lighting. The final render is produced after all elements meet client expectations.
